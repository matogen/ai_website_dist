Capabilities

Consulting

Matogen Applied Insights, with 7 PhDs on our team and 400+ years of combined experience, is expertly positioned to help organizations solve complex business problems and achieve significant results.

Data Science

Matogen Applied Insights has a team of highly skilled data scientists who leverage international best practices to create powerful predictive models that increase profitability and competitiveness. Our team has produced a range of cutting-edge solutions within the finance, agriculture and health sectors.

Data Analytics

Data is explored and visualised efficiently and effectively to uncover trends and impactful insights. Our team is also actively involved in clients’ business intelligence operations to facilitate executive decision-making.

Data Architecture & Data Engineering

For companies who have their own modelling teams, Matogen Applied Insights is adept in combining and enriching raw data in innovative ways to create new features rendering for advanced analytics. In addition, we can provide expert guidelines on data pipelines, storage and processing.

Systems Solutions

Capitalising on our foundation as a software development company, Matogen Applied Insights is in a strong position to offer an interdisciplinary approach to conceptualise and construct data-driven business solutions, ranging from highly customisable dashboards as powerful information management tools to bespoke data analytics pipelines.

Artificial Intelligence

Matogen Applied Insights has spearheaded groundbreaking AI projects ranging from computer vision in the agriculture industry, neural nets, support vector machines and extreme boosted trees for finance, to fitting a compartmental model for covid data.
